Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Macaroni and Cheese is a wonderful food originating from Italy.
It is a very simple dish that is now eaten around the world.
The cheese is very creamy and delightful in texture.
To top it off, the unique and robust shape of the macaroni makes this dish unforgettable.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Box Elbow Macaroni
  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1/4 Cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 2 Cups Milk
  • 2 Cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Grounded Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Bring to a boil a large pot of lightly salted water.
    Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water and stir occassionally
    until cooked through but firm to the bite. It should take 8 minutes.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat while doing step one
  3. Put in flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth. It should take 5 minutes.
  4. Put milk in slowly, while stirring. Cook and stir until mixture
    is smooth and bubbling and make sure the milk doesn't burn.
    It should take 5 minutes.
  5. Put Cheddar Cheese in and stir until melted. It should take 2-4 minutes
  6. Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until coated.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
