Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is probably the most popular food around the world.
It is renowned for being very inexpensive and can be used to supplement many meals
Rice has the ability to harden to preserve itself.
This is the reason why fried rice is normally made with leftover rice.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 Chopped Baby Carrots
  • 1/2 Cup Frozen Green Peas
  • 2 Tablespoons Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Clove Garlic, Minced
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 3 Cups Leftover Cooked White Rice
  • 1 Tablespoon Soy Sauce
  • 2 Teaspoons Seasame Oil

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Place carrots in a small saucespan and cover with water.
    Bring to a low boil and cook for 3-5 minutes. Stir in peas
    then drain in a colander.
  3. Heat a wok over high heat. Put in vegetable oil and stir
    carrots, peas, and garlic, then cook for 30 seconds.
    Add eggs and stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables.
  4. Put in cooked rice and add soy sauce. Toss rice to coat.
    Drizzle with seasame oil and toss again.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
