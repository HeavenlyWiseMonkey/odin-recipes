Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

Chicken noodle soup is a nice and easy dish to make.
It is very enjoyable for friends and family with its warmth and flavours.
As well as, utilizes inexpensive and tasty ingredients you can find in all supermarkets.
This soup really fills you up and reminds you of home.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Broth
  • Onions
  • Celery
  • Noodles
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Carrots
