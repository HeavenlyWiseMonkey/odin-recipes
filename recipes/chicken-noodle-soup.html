Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

Chicken noodle soup is a nice and easy dish to make.
It is very enjoyable for friends and family with its warmth and flavours.
As well as, utilizes inexpensive and tasty ingredients you can find in all supermarkets.
This soup really fills you up and reminds you of home.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb Chopped Cooked Chicken Breast
  • 14.5 oz Chicken Broth
  • 14.5 oz Vegetable Broth
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Onions
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Celery
  • 1 1/2 Cups Egg Noodles
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Dried Basil
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Dried Oregano
  • 1 Cup Sliced Carrots
  • 1 Tablespoon Butter

Steps

  1. Put butter in a big pot over medium heat. Then, put onion and celery in and cook until tender (5 minutes).
  2. Put chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodle, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper in. Stir and bring to a boil.
  3. Lower heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
